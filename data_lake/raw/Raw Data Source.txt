Raw data files are not included in this submission due to their large size. 
Below are the data sources and direct links used in this project.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
1. BRFSS 2024 (Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System)
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Source: Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)
Format: SAS Transport File (.XPT)
Description: Contains individual-level survey responses on health behaviors,
lifestyle factors, chronic disease conditions, and demographics.

Public download link:
https://www.cdc.gov/brfss/annual_data/annual_2024.html
